Help & Support

Welcome to the Life With Logic Help page. Here you can find answers to common questions and get support related to your account, projects, and job opportunities.

Account & Login Issues

If you are unable to log in or facing authentication problems, please make sure you are using the correct email and login method. Our platform uses secure Firebase authentication.

Projects Help

To create or post a project, login is required. After logging in, go to the Projects section and click on Create Project. Add your project details, image, and deployed link carefully.

Jobs & Internships

Life With Logic provides worldwide job and internship listings, including remote and full-time opportunities. Users can search jobs and apply directly using external links.

Privacy & Security

Your data privacy is important to us. All user data is handled securely and we do not misuse personal information. Please refer to our Privacy Policy page for detailed information.
